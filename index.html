AI In Practice

What AI Integration Looks Like in Your Business

Real ways Copilot Studio agents and generative AI slot into the systems you already use.

Customer Support Agent

A Copilot Studio agent answers customer questions and resolves common tickets 24/7 on your website, Teams, or WhatsApp — escalating to a human only when it needs to.

Internal Knowledge Assistant

Employees ask HR, IT, and policy questions in Teams and get instant, accurate answers pulled directly from your SharePoint, Dataverse, and internal documents.

Sales & CRM Assistant

AI qualifies inbound leads, drafts personalised follow-ups, and keeps Dynamics 365 or your CRM updated automatically — so your sales team spends time selling, not typing.

Document & Invoice Processing

AI Builder and generative AI read invoices, contracts, and forms, extract the data you need, and route it into your finance or ERP system automatically.

Meeting & Reporting Insights

Generative AI summarises meetings and turns raw Power BI data into plain-language reports and dashboards your team can act on without a data analyst.

Employee Onboarding Agent

New hires get guided through setup and policy questions by a Copilot agent, while Power Automate quietly provisions accounts, access, and equipment in the background.
